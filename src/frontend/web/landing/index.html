What it is

This is a tool to help you understand how different decisions and scenarios can affect your long-term financial future, within the context of the Irish tax system.

What it's not

This is not a budgeting tool nor financial advice. Don't make serious life decisions based on this. It's not a substitute for a good financial advisor.

Key Features

Scenario Planning

Model your financial future with customizable life events and economic conditions.

Stress Testing

Test your financial plan against market crashes and varying economic conditions.

Monte Carlo Simulation

Understand the probability of success with volatility-based simulations.

Save Scenarios

Save and reload your financial scenarios for future reference and comparison.
